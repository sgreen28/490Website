The Voluntary Extra Time (VET) Application

About the VET application redefines shift management by providing a seamless experience for employees seeking additional work opportunities. Through its user-friendly interface, VET empowers individuals to explore available shifts and easily sign up, fostering a sense of autonomy in their scheduling. The platform not only enhances transparency by offering detailed insights into hours worked and earnings but also prioritizes user experience across all levels of technical expertise. By streamlining administrative tasks for employers, VET contributes to a more efficient workplace, ultimately supporting a healthier work-life balance for all employees.
